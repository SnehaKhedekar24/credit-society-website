About Our Credit Society

Trimurti Co-Operative Credit Society is a trusted financial institution built on the principles of cooperation, transparency, and community support. Since 1982, we have been empowering members with secure savings and affordable loans.

Our Ambitions

  • Promote financial inclusion for all community members
  • Encourage savings habits and long-term wealth creation
  • Provide affordable loans for education, housing, and business
  • Support senior citizens with special deposit and loan benefits
  • Invest in community development projects
