Understanding Interest

Interest is the cost of borrowing money or the return earned on deposits. It is calculated based on the principal amount, rate of interest, and time period. Our society offers attractive interest rates on deposits and affordable rates on loans.

Reducing Balance Interest

In the reducing balance method, interest is charged only on the outstanding loan balance. As you repay your loan in installments, the principal reduces, and so does the interest charged. This means your interest burden decreases over time, making it a fair and transparent way of calculating loan repayments.

  • ✅ Interest is calculated on remaining balance, not the original loan amount
  • ✅ Each EMI reduces both principal and interest
  • ✅ More cost-effective compared to flat interest method
